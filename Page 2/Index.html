Phillipines is one of the best areas to tour when you wanna tour around the world.

The country has so many beautiful places to explore some of them can be mountains, Oceans,

Structures, and more. They have so much cultural places too, and even historical areas to see

Here are atleast 6 areas you should atleast visit!

Mayon Volcano

The Mayon Volcano is a very old volcano that destroyed nearby towns and even destroyed the baroque church, It last erupted in 1814

Puerto Princesa Underground River

This tourism spot is a underground river where you go through a cave with a paddle boat.

Chocolate Hills

Hills that look like chocolate, they are a amazing area to see since the Chocolate hills literally is an area that the hills look like chocolate. (Ps: Dont eat it please)

Calle Crisologo, Vigan

A town that is stuck in the 16th centuary, it's a nice place to sight see and even better at night.

Tubbataha Reef, Palawan

A coral reef, it's a top spot for divers around the world, its a very beautiful to see too, a lot fishes to even identify.

Kawasan Falls, Cebu

a beautiful area with water falls and a lake in the bottom a very good natural area to swim in and enjoy yourself.
